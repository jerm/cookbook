Sweet Potato Muffins

1/2     Cup Butter, softened (1 stick)
1 1/4   Cups sugar
2       eggs
1 1/4   Cups cooked, mashed sweet potatoes (1 large sweet potato - about 1 lbs.)
1/4 - 1/3 Cup milk
1 1/2   Cups flour
2       tsp. baking powder
1/4     tsp. salt
1       tsp. cinnamon
1/4     tsp. ground nutmeg
1/2     Cup raisins
1/4     Cup chopped pecans

Topping:
2       TB sugar
1/4     tsp. cinnamon

Preheat ovent to 375 degrees.  Cream the butter until light and fluffy.  Gradually add the sugar, beating on medium speed.  Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.  Stir in the sweet potatoes and milk.  In a separate bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg.  Add the creamed mixture, stirring just until moistened.  Fold in the raisins and pecans.  Spoon the batter intogreased muffin pans, filling 2/3 full. Mix together the sugar and cinnamon and sprinkle liberally over each muffin.  Bake at 375 degrees for 2-27 minutes.  

Prep time:  20 minutes (not including time to cook sweet potatoes)
Baking time:  25-27 minutes
Yield:  20-22 muffins
