CHICKEN METHI
Sun dried fenugreek leaves gives this chcken dish an aromatic taste.
 
Ingredients
 	 
   Chicken 	750 grams
   Onions 	3 medium
   Ginger 	2 inch piece
   Green chilli 	3
   Fresh coriander leaves 	a few
   Oil 	2 tablespoons
   Bay leaves 	2 leaves
   Green cardamom 	5
   Black cardamom 	2
   Cloves 	3
   Cinnamon 	1 inch stick
   Black peppercorns 	5
   Garlic paste 	2 tablespoons
   Turmeric powder 	1/2 teaspoon
   Red chilli powder 	1 teaspoon
   Coriander powder 	1 tablespoon
   Yogurt 	1 cup
   Dry fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi) 	2 tablespoons
   Garam masala powder 	1 teaspoon
   Salt 	to taste
 
Directions:
 
Clean, skin, wash and cut chicken into medium sized pieces.

Peel, wash and chop onions. Peel, wash and chop ginger. Remove stems, wash and chop green chillies. Wash and chop coriander leaves.

Heat oil in a pan. Add bay leaves, green cardamoms, black cardamoms, cloves, cinnamon and peppercorns. Saute until cardamoms start to crackle.

Add onions and cook until translucent and soft. Make sure to stir continuously. Add ginger, garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, coriander powder and chopped green chillies. Saute for a minute.

Add chicken pieces and beaten yogurt and cook on high heat for seven to eight minutes. Add kasoori methi and three-fourth cup of water. Cover the pan and cook on low heat for ten minutes. Add garam masala powder, salt and mix properly. Serve hot garnished with chopped fresh coriander leaves.
