Barbecue Seasoning Rub

INGREDIENTS

For spice barbecue rub:

  1/4 cup red chile powder (like New Mexico or Ancho)
  1/4 cup dark brown sugar
  1/4 cup kosher salt
  2 tbsp  hot chili powder (arbol/cayenne) (adjust up/down for desired heat level)
  2 tbsp  paprika
  2 tbsp  garlic powder
  1 tbsp  cumin powder
  1 tbsp  mustard powder
  1 tbsp  oregano
  1 tbsp  ground black pepper

DIRECTIONS:

Combine ingredients and mix.  Store in air-tight container until use.
