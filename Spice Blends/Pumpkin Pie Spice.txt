Pumpkin Pie Spice

Ingredients

  1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  1/8 teaspoon ground cloves

Directions

In a small bowl, mix together cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger and cloves. Store in an airtight container.
