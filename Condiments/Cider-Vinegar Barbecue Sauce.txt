Cider-Vinegar Barbecue Sauce

Ingredients

  1 1/2 cups cider vinegar
  1 cup yellow or brown mustard
  1/2 cup ketchup
  1/3 cup packed brown sugar
  2 garlic cloves, smashed
  1 teaspoon kosher salt
  1 teaspoon cayenne
  1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Directions

Combine the vinegar, mustard, ketchup, brown sugar, garlic, salt, cayenne, and black pepper in a saucepan over medium heat. Simmer gently, stirring, for 10 minutes until the sugar dissolves. Take it off the heat, cool and refridgerate.
