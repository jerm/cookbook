By Me

Ingredients:

  potatoes
  rosemary
  olive oil (or some other type)
  salt (1/2 tablespoon)
  pepper (1/2 tablespoon)
  rosemary (~3 tablespoons fresh (at least) or 1 tablespoon dried)


Preparation:

Cut the potatos into small pieces.  Mince the rosemary.

Cooking: 

Heat a pan, put some oil in the pan.  Add the potatos to the pan.  Sprinkle the
salt, pepper and rosemary over the potatos.  Stir and cook until golden brown.
