CAJUN SEASONING

    1 tbsp.     sweet paprika
    2 1/2 tsp.  salt
    1 tsp.      onion powder
    1 tsp.      garlic powder
    1 tsp.      ground red pepper
    3/4 tsp.    white pepper
    3/4 tsp.    black pepper
    1/2 tsp.    dried thyme leaves
    1/2 tsp.    dried oregano leaves

Mix and store in air-tight container.
