Nathalie's French Vinaigrette

Ingredients

  1 cup oil (vegetable, olive, or other)
  1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  1.5 tbsp mustard
  1 tsp salt
  1 tsp ground black pepper
  1/2 tsp basil

Directions

Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Whisk together. Store in a container in the refrigerator. Shake to mix before use.
