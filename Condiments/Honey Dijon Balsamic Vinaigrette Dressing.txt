Honey Dijon Balsamic Vinaigrette

Ingredients

  1/3 cup balsamic vinegar
  1/2 cup olive oil
  2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  1 tablespoon honey
  salt and pepper to taste

Directions

Whisk balsamic vinegar, olive oil, mustard, honey, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Place in container for storage in the fridge until use.
