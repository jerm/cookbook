Sweet Curry Powder

A simple curry powder goes well with vegetables such as potatoes or cauliflower.


I N G R E D I E N T S
1 tsp ground coriander
1 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp turmeric
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground cloves
1/4 tsp cayenne

I N S T R U C T I O N S
Combine ingredients and mix well.  Mixture can be made in larger batches.  Store in a tightly sealed container for up to 2 months.
