Tomato Ketchup

INGREDIENTS

  4 pints ripe grape tomatoes, cut in half
  2 cups red wine vinegar
  2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  4 teaspoons salt
  2 teaspoons black pepper
  3/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce.

PREPARATION

1. In a wide skillet, simmer tomatoes, vinegar, sugar, salt and pepper until a thick jamlike mixture forms and liquid evaporates, 20 to 25 minutes. Pur√©e until smooth. Strain through a fine-mesh sieve. (For a perfectly smooth texture, strain twice.) Stir in Worcestershire. Chill before serving.

YIELD About 2 cups.
