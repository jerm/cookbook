Pumpkin Pie with Brown Sugar-Walnut Topping

Topping:

    1/2 cup walnut pieces
    1/4 cup (packed) golden brown sugar
    1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
    Pinch of fine sea salt

For filling:

    1 cup (packed) golden brown sugar
    1 1/2 tbsp flour
    2 large eggs
    1/2 tsp fine sea salt
    1 tsp ground cinnamon
    1/2 tsp ground ginger
    1/2 tsp ground cloves
    1/2 tsp ground allspice
    1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
    1/2 tsp ground cardamom
    1 cup pumpkin puree
    1 cup heavy whipping cream

Directions

For topping:
Combine all ingredients. Using on/off turns, blend to fine crumbs.

For pie:
Position oven rack in bottom third of oven; preheat to 350°F. 

Whisk brown sugar, flour, eggs, sea salt, and spices in medium bowl. Add pumpkin and cream and whisk until well blended and smooth.

Pour filling into pie pan. Bake pie until filling is firm, about 30 minutes.

Sprinkle topping evenly over top of pie. Reduce oven temperature to 325°F; continue to bake pie until filling is set and slightly puffed in center, about 15 minutes longer. Transfer pie to rack and cool completely.
