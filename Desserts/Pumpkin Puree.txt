Pumpkin Puree

Prep time: 30 minutes
Cook time: 1 hour

Ingredients:

  1 sugar pumpkin

Directions:

1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C)

2. Cut the pumpkin in half, stem to base. Remove seeds and stringy pulp. Cover each half with foil.

3. Bake in preheated oven, foil side up, 1 hour or until tender

4. Scrape pumpkin meat from shell halves and puree in a blender. Strain to remove any remaining stringy pieces. Store in the freezer in freezer safe bags.
