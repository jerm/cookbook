Chai Cookies

Ingredients

    * 2 sticks butter
    * 1 1/3 cups dark brown sugar
    * 2 cups all-purpose flour
    * 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
    * 1/2 teaspoon cardamom
    * 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
    * 1/2 teaspoon cloves
    * 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
    * 1/4 cup crystalized ginger, chopped

Directions

Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.

Cream butter and sugar until fluffy. Sift flour and baking powder.  Add flour, backing powder cardamom, black pepper, cloves and ground ginger into the creamed butter and blend well. Fold cyrstalized ginger into batter. Make into 24 even balls and place on a parchment-lined baking sheet. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes or until golden brown on the edges.
