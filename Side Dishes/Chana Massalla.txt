Chana Massalla (Chick Peas)

Ingredients

  Serves 4

    * 1 Cup chick peas (dried or tinned)
    * Oil (vegetable, olive, butter or ...)
    * Half an onion finely chopped
    * 2 inches grated root ginger
    * 4 cloves garlic sliced
    * 2 teaspoons curry powder
    * 1 teaspoon chili powder
    * 1 teaspoon cumin powder or seeds
    * 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
    * Half an onion chopped in large chunks
    * half a green pepper finely chopped
    * 2 Tablespoons minced tomatoes (or tomato puree)
    * Half cup coarsly chopped tomatoes
    * 2 teaspoons sugar
    * 5 tablespoons chopped cilantro
    
Method

If using dried chick peas then you will need to follow the instructions on soaking before use.

Fry the finely chopped onion until translucent in the oil.  Add the curry powder, chili powder, cumin, turmeric, sliced garlic and grated ginger and stir fry for a few minutes.  Add the pepper, chopped tomatoes, minced tomatoes, chunks of onion, and sugar and stir fry for a few minutes. Add the chick peas and stir fry for a few minutes.  Serve topped with the cilantro!
