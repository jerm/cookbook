Carrot Bread

2       Cups sugar
1 1/2   Cups vegetable oil
4       Eggs
3       Cups all-purpose flour
2       tsp. baking powder
2       tsp. baking soda
2       tsp. cinnamon
1/2     tsp. salt
2       Cups shredded carrots (4-5 medium carrots)
1       Cup chopped nuts (optional)

Preheat oven to 375 degrees for bread or 350 degrees for muffins.  Cream together the sugar and the oil.  Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addtion.  Gradually add the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt and mix well.  Pour into 2 medium greased and floured loaf pans and bake at 375 degrees for 55 minutes.  This batter can also be used to make carrot muffins.  For muffins, grease the tins with shortening or vegetable oil, fill 2/3 full and bake at 350 degrees for 25-28 minutes.

Prep time:  15 minutes
Baking time:  55 minutes
Yield:  2 loaves or 24-26 muffins
