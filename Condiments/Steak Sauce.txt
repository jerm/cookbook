Steak Sauce

Ingredients

 1 1/4 cups ketchup
 2 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard
 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
 1 1/2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
 4 drops hot pepper sauce (e.g. Tabascoâ„¢)

Directions
